Our Services

At Max’s Handyman Services, we know that your home is your sanctuary, and maintaining it should be stress-free. With a focus on quality craftsmanship and attention to detail, we provide a wide range of reliable handyman solutions, from plumbing and electrical work to carpentry and home repairs. Whether it’s a minor fix or a major project, our handymen are dedicated to delivering professional, timely services that meet your needs and exceed your expectations.

Plumbing

We offer a wide range of plumbing services, including fixing leaks, repairing broken pipes, unclogging drains, and installing new fixtures. Whether it’s a small drip or a major leak, our handymen are equipped to handle it all.

Electrical Work

We provide expert electrical services such as fixing faulty wiring, installing new lighting, and troubleshooting electrical issues. Safety is a priority, ensuring all repairs and installations are up to code.

Carpentry

Our carpentry services include building shelves, repairing doors, and installing trim. Whether you need custom work or general repairs, we brings quality craftsmanship to every project.

Painting and Drywall Repair

Our handymen specializes in painting rooms, touch-ups, and drywall repair. Whether you're refreshing a room or fixing damaged walls, we ensure a smooth, professional finish every time.

Furniture Assembly

From complex IKEA furniture to custom pieces, we can quickly and efficiently assemble all types of furniture. Let him take the hassle out of putting together your new purchases.

Gutter Cleaning and Repair

We offer gutter cleaning and repair services to ensure your home’s drainage system works efficiently. Avoid damage to your home by keeping your gutters in top shape.
